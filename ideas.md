# FlashPreviews Consulting Group — Design Ideas

Premium consulting firm specializing in programmatic SEO, lead generation engines, and automated client acquisition systems. Target audience: attorneys, real estate professionals, business owners. Goal: trust, authority, conversion.

## Idea A — "Private Equity Editorial"

**Design Movement:** Swiss International Typographic Style meets Private Equity firm aesthetics (think Blackstone, KKR)

**Core Principles:**
- Extreme typographic hierarchy: massive display numbers and headlines dominate
- Negative space as a power signal — emptiness communicates confidence
- Data-first storytelling: metrics are the hero, not imagery
- Strict grid discipline with intentional breaks for emphasis

**Color Philosophy:**
Off-white (#F8F7F4) background with near-black (#0E0E0E) text. Single gold accent (#B8963E) used only for numbers, borders, and CTAs. No gradients. Color restraint signals premium positioning.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Asymmetric editorial grid. Headlines bleed left, stats anchor right. Sections separated by thin 1px gold rules rather than background color changes. Full-bleed text sections alternate with tight stat columns.

**Signature Elements:**
- Oversized numerals (30,000+) displayed at 120px+ as visual anchors
- Thin horizontal gold rule lines as section dividers
- Serif display font (Playfair Display) for headlines, sans-serif (DM Sans) for body

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Minimal. Hover states reveal gold underlines on links. CTA buttons use border-only style that fills on hover. No scroll animations — the content speaks.

**Animation:**
Fade-in on scroll for stat counters only. No parallax, no slides. Counter animation for metrics (0 → 30,000+).

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display Bold — headlines, stat numbers
- Body: DM Sans Regular/Medium — body copy, labels
- Accent: DM Mono — technical labels, case study data points

## Idea B — "Dark Authority"

**Design Movement:** Bloomberg Terminal meets high-end law firm — dark, data-dense, commanding

**Core Principles:**
- Dark backgrounds signal expertise and seriousness
- Gold accents create premium contrast without warmth
- Monospaced type for data points signals technical precision
- Structured information hierarchy: no decoration, only function

**Color Philosophy:**
Near-black (#0A0A0A) background. Warm white (#F0EDE8) for primary text. Gold (#C9A84C) for accents, borders, CTAs. Dark gray (#1A1A1A) for card backgrounds. The darkness communicates that this firm operates at a level most don't reach.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Full-width sections with tight internal columns. Hero is full-viewport with centered headline. Stats section uses a 4-column grid of bordered boxes. Case study uses a left-anchored timeline layout.

**Signature Elements:**
- Gold horizontal lines as decorative separators
- Bordered metric cards with gold top-border accent
- Monospaced font for all numbers and technical data

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Subtle. Gold border appears on card hover. CTA button fills from left on hover. Smooth scroll between sections.

**Animation:**
Entrance animations only on first load (fade up, 300ms). Counter animation for metrics. No continuous animations.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Cormorant Garamond Bold Italic — hero headlines
- Body: Outfit Regular — body copy
- Data: JetBrains Mono — all numbers, metrics, technical labels

## Idea C — "Clean White Authority" (SELECTED)

**Design Movement:** High-end management consulting meets editorial publishing — McKinsey meets The Economist

**Core Principles:**
- White space is the primary design element — every element earns its place
- Typography does the heavy lifting: size, weight, and tracking create hierarchy
- Results are the product: metrics displayed with editorial confidence
- Conversion through clarity: the user always knows what to do next

**Color Philosophy:**
Pure white (#FFFFFF) background. Near-black (#111111) for primary text. Warm dark gray (#444444) for secondary text. Gold (#B8860B) used sparingly for accent lines, CTA borders, and key numbers. One accent, used with discipline.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Left-aligned editorial layout. Headlines are left-justified and large. Content flows in clear vertical sections with generous padding. Stats use a horizontal band with large numbers. Case study uses a two-column layout: narrative left, data right.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin gold left-border on blockquotes and key callouts
- Large, bold metric numbers with small descriptive labels below
- Section headers use small-caps tracking with a thin gold underline rule

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Purposeful. Every interactive element has a clear affordance. CTA buttons are solid black with white text — no ambiguity. Hover states are subtle (slight background shift, underline reveal).

**Animation:**
Minimal. Fade-in on scroll for sections (opacity 0→1, translateY 20px→0, 400ms ease). Counter animation for key metrics. No decorative animations.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Cormorant Garamond Bold — hero headlines, section titles
- Body: DM Sans Regular/Medium — all body copy, navigation, labels
- Mono: JetBrains Mono — metric numbers, data labels, case study figures

## Selected: Idea C — "Clean White Authority"

This approach best serves the conversion goal. White authority signals confidence without intimidation. The editorial layout guides the eye naturally toward CTAs. Gold accents create premium positioning without ostentation.
